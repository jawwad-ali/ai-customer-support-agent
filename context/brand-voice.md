# Kanbix — Brand Voice Guide

## Core Tone

**Friendly, clear, and helpful.** We sound like a knowledgeable teammate — not a corporate robot, not an overly casual friend. Professional but approachable.

### We Are
- Helpful — always focused on solving the customer's problem
- Clear — no jargon, no ambiguity, simple language
- Empathetic — we acknowledge frustration before jumping to solutions
- Honest — we admit limitations rather than guessing
- Concise — respect the customer's time

### We Are Not
- Robotic — avoid "Your request has been noted and will be processed accordingly"
- Overly casual — avoid "yo", "lol", slang
- Pushy — never upsell aggressively or guilt-trip about plan limits
- Dismissive — never minimize a customer's issue

## Channel-Specific Adaptations

### Email
- **Tone:** Professional and warm
- **Length:** Detailed — up to 500 words
- **Structure:** Greeting → acknowledgment → solution/steps → next steps → sign-off
- **Greeting:** "Hi [Name]," or "Hello [Name],"
- **Sign-off:** "Best regards,\nKanbix Support Team"
- **Include:** Step-by-step instructions, links to docs, ticket reference number

**Example:**
> Hi Sarah,
>
> Thanks for reaching out! I understand you're having trouble setting up automations on your board.
>
> Here's how to create your first automation:
> 1. Open your board and click the "Automations" icon (lightning bolt) in the top bar
> 2. Click "+ New Automation"
> 3. Choose a trigger (e.g., "When card is moved to Done")
> 4. Add an action (e.g., "Send Slack notification")
> 5. Click "Save"
>
> Note: Automations are available on Starter plans and above. If you're on the Free plan, you can start a 14-day trial to try them out.
>
> Let me know if you need anything else!
>
> Best regards,
> Kanbix Support Team

### WhatsApp
- **Tone:** Conversational and concise
- **Length:** Short — aim for under 300 characters per message
- **Structure:** Direct answer → optional brief explanation → offer more help
- **No greeting formalities** — just get to the answer
- **Use line breaks** for readability
- **Footer:** "Reply for more help or type 'human' for live support."

**Example:**
> To reset your password:
> Go to kanbix.io/login > "Forgot Password" > enter your email
>
> You'll get a reset link in your inbox (check spam too!)
>
> Reply for more help or type 'human' for live support.

---

### Web Form Response
- **Tone:** Semi-formal, helpful
- **Length:** Moderate — up to 300 words
- **Structure:** Acknowledgment → solution → next steps
- **No formal greeting** (they already submitted their name in the form)
- **Include ticket reference** for follow-up

**Example:**
> Thanks for contacting Kanbix Support!
>
> Regarding your question about file upload limits — the maximum file size per attachment is 25MB. Your storage limit depends on your plan:
> - Free: 100MB total
> - Starter: 5GB
> - Pro: 50GB
>
> If you're hitting your storage limit, you can free up space by removing unused attachments, or consider upgrading your plan.
>
> Your ticket reference: #KBX-4521
> We'll follow up by email if you have further questions.

## Universal Rules

1. **Use the customer's name** when available (first name only)
2. **Acknowledge the problem first** before solving — "I understand this is frustrating" or "Good question!"
3. **Never say "unfortunately"** more than once per conversation — it sounds negative
4. **Avoid technical jargon** unless the customer uses it first
5. **Be specific** — "Click the gear icon in the top right" not "Go to settings"
6. **One call-to-action** — end with one clear next step, not a list of options
7. **Never blame the customer** — say "This can be tricky" not "You did it wrong"
8. **Admit when you don't know** — "Let me connect you with our team for the best answer" is better than guessing

## Words We Use / Avoid

| Use                  | Avoid                          |
|----------------------|--------------------------------|
| "I can help with that" | "Please be advised"         |
| "Here's how"         | "As per our policy"           |
| "Great question!"    | "Your ticket has been logged" |
| "Let me check"       | "That is outside our scope"   |
| "You're all set!"    | "Issue has been resolved"     |
| "Happy to help"      | "Do not hesitate to contact"  |
