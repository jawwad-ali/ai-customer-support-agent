## Key Features

1. **Kanban Boards** — Drag-and-drop task management with customizable columns
2. **Task Cards** — Descriptions, checklists, attachments, comments, due dates, assignees, labels
3. **Workflow Automations** — Rule-based triggers and actions (e.g., "When card moves to Done, notify Slack channel")
4. **Time Tracking** — Built-in timer on cards, timesheet reports
5. **Integrations** — Slack, GitHub, Google Drive, Zapier, Webhooks
6. **Team Collaboration** — Real-time updates, @mentions, activity feed
7. **Reporting Dashboards** — Burndown charts, team velocity, workload distribution
8. **Templates** — Pre-built board templates (Sprint Planning, Content Calendar, Client Onboarding, etc.)
9. **Guest Access** — Invite external collaborators with limited permissions
10. **Mobile App** — iOS and Android (view boards, update cards, receive notifications)
